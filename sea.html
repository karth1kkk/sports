SEA Games

International Level Competition


The SEA Games has been our club's ultimate competition since the APU Badminton Club's founding. Come along for a thrilling ride as we take the big stage at the SEA Games and make a lasting impression with our everlasting commitment to and love of badminton. Watch our exciting matches, which are characterised by sportsmanship and collaboration, as we compete to win on one of the biggest badminton stages in the world. Join us as we persistently ascend and dutifully represent APUBC at every SEA Games event.

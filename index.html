About

APU's Badminton Club is a vibrant online platform dedicated to the sport of badminton, catering to enthusiasts within the APU community. The website serves as a hub for members to stay updated on club events, schedule matches, and foster a sense of camaraderie among badminton aficionados.

Visitors can find information on upcoming tournaments, practice sessions, and engage in discussions about the sport. APU's Badminton Club website is not only a practical tool for coordinating activities but also a virtual space that cultivates a passion for badminton and fosters connections among like-minded individuals at the university.

Supervisor Karthik Trainer Connor
